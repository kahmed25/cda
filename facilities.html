Facilities

BED & ROOM CATEGORIES

You may choose from different categories of in-patient beds/rooms of the hospital, including Ward, Double Bed, Standard, private & deluxe Single and Standard & VIP / Deluxe Suite . Beds/Cabins are allotted depending on your choice and availability. We also have Day Care Unit for patients who have had out-patient procedures, as well ten specialized state-of-the-art Intensive Care Unit (ICU), Critical Care Units (CCU), High Dependency Unit (HDU), Neonatal Intensive Care unit (NICU) & Paediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU) for management of critically ill patients.

NURSING CARE

We Care Hospital Limited provides 24-hour comprehensive, individualized nursing care for in-patients based on their assessed nursing needs. All our nurses are well-qualified and experienced with a commitment to patient care. To provide smooth In Patient Nursing services there are 02 Nurses Stations are available where a group of experienced nurses are available 24/7

FOOD & BEVERAGE

Diet is an integral part of patient medication. Therefore, food from outside source is discouraged. Dieticians, in association with your doctor, will assess your diet needs. You are advised to follow the dieting instructions specified by them for speedy recovery. Religious sentiments of the patients will be considered while deciding on the diets. Kindly inform the dietician of the same.

HOUSEKEEPING & LAUNDRY

We Care Hospital Limited maintains the highest level of cleanliness. Housekeeping Department looks after the cleanliness of your room. Wards are cleaned and bed linen and towels are changed on a daily basis. All your laundry is processed in our in-house laundry service. We understand the importance of infection control practices for handling, processing, storing and delivery of textiles in a healthcare setting.

GUEST SERVICES

Attendants and all other visitors can also buy meals and refreshments from United Hospital Cafeteria (Ground Floor), open from 8:30 am to 10 pm every day. Cafeteria offers hygienic and tasty local and continental cuisine, including soups, salads, snacks, tandoori, grills, ice-creams, milk shakes, cocktails, and fresh juices
